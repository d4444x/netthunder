Net Thunder provides a highly scalable and secure enterprise/data center in a box. Net-Thunder allows for cloud style services in your own datacenter while still future-proofing your infrastructure.

We provide an open source virtualization operating system, LDAP Directory/SSO services, and secure client management. We also provide security/network consulting, pre-built enterprise applications, and an enterprise app development platform.
